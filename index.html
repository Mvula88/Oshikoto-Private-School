About Our School

Nurturing Young Minds Since 2025

Oshikoto Private School is dedicated to providing quality early childhood education in a nurturing and stimulating environment. Located in Ehenye, Oshakati, we are committed to helping every child reach their full potential.

Our experienced educators use modern teaching methods combined with traditional values to ensure your child receives the best foundation for their educational journey.
